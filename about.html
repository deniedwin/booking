About Klif

Klif Est OÜ is a soon-to-be incorporated company specialized in simplifying global payments for outdoor rock climbing tours. We operate alongside Klif Curacao BV to provide a seamless, brutalist approach to booking and beyond.

Our mission is to offer a raw, minimal, and straightforward experience for climbing enthusiasts who want to focus on the adventure—not the paperwork.
